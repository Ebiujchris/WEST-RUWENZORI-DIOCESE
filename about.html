About the Diocese

West Ruwenzori Diocese — rooted in faith, serving Bundibugyo District since August 2023.

Our Story

A New Diocese,
A New Beginning

The West Ruwenzori Diocese was carved out from the larger Ruwenzori Diocese on August 27, 2023, becoming the 38th Diocese in the Anglican Province of the Church of Uganda. This historic step was taken to bring pastoral care closer to believers in the rugged, underserved regions of Bundibugyo District.

Prior to its formation, faithful members often endured arduous journeys for spiritual support — a challenge that has now been alleviated under the leadership of the pioneer Bishop, the Rt. Rev. Barnabas Tibaijuka.

The diocese is headquartered at St. Barnabas Cathedral on Bumadu Hill, Bundibugyo Town Council, Bwamba County. It serves as a spiritual home and center for the entire Bundibugyo community.
