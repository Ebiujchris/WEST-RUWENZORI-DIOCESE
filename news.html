Anniversary
August 28, 2025 · By Edward Kyomuhendo

Joyous Welcome for Archbishop Kaziimba as West Ruwenzori Diocese Marks Two Years of Faith and Growth

Bundibugyo, Uganda — In a vibrant display of unity and devotion, the Christians of West Ruwenzori Diocese extended a heartfelt welcome to the Most Rev. Dr. Stephen Samuel Kaziimba Mugalu, Archbishop of the Church of Uganda, during his pastoral visit. The visit culminated in a thanksgiving service on August 27, commemorating the second anniversary of the diocese's establishment and the episcopal ministry of its pioneering Bishop, the Rt. Rev. Barnabas Tibaijuka.

The event, held at St. Barnabas Cathedral in Bumadu, was a testament to the high spirits and unwavering faith of the local Christian community, who gathered in thousands to celebrate their shared journey "Rooted in Christ, Growing in Faith."

Archbishop Kaziimba arrived in the diocese on the afternoon of August 26, greeted by enthusiastic crowds of believers, clergy, and community leaders. The air was filled with songs of praise and traditional dances, reflecting the deep joy and anticipation that had built up in the lead-up to this milestone occasion. For many attendees, the Archbishop's presence symbolized not just ecclesiastical authority but a beacon of hope and guidance for a diocese still in its formative years.

Christians from remote villages traveled long distances to participate, their faces alight with smiles and their voices raised in harmonious worship, embodying a collective spirit of resilience and optimism. The West Ruwenzori Diocese, carved out from the larger Ruwenzori Diocese on August 27, 2023, was created to bring pastoral care closer to believers in the rugged, underserved regions of Bundibugyo District. The two-year mark served as a poignant reminder of God's faithfulness.

History
May 21, 2024

The Inauguration of West Ruwenzori Diocese: A New Era for Bundibugyo

In a historic ceremony that drew together political, religious, and community leaders, the West Ruwenzori Diocese in Bundibugyo District was officially established with the consecration and enthronement of its first bishop, Rt. Rev. Barnabas Tibaijuka. The event took place at the St. Barnabas Cathedral in Bumadu and marked a significant milestone for the Church of Uganda.

The consecration service was led by the Archbishop of the Church of Uganda, The Most Rev. Dr. Stephen Samuel Kaziimba Mugalu, who emphasized the importance of unity and support within the diocese. Hon. Col. Tom Butiime, the Minister of Tourism, Wildlife, and Antiquities, represented President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni as the Chief Guest, highlighting the government's recognition of the diocese's role in fostering community development and spiritual growth.

Archbishop Kaziimba Mugalu assured Bishop Tibaijuka of the church's prayers and support in his new mission. Bishop Tibaijuka called upon Christians and clergy to embrace patriotism and hard work. He confirmed 84 new members into the Christian faith during his inaugural pastoral visit to All Saints Parish Church in Bundibugyo town.

Hon. Richard Gafabusa Muhumuza, Member of Parliament for Bwamba County, pledged political backing to ensure the diocese meets its objectives. With Bishop Tibaijuka's leadership, the diocese aims to foster spiritual growth, community development, and national patriotism — and the enthusiastic response from the community suggests a very promising future ahead.
