Message from the Bishop

A Foundation Built
on Faith

Receive Christian and lovely greetings from the Diocese of West Ruwenzori — from my wife Alice Tibaijuka, myself, our family, and the entire Christian community at large.

We welcome you to this site. As the pioneer bishop of West Ruwenzori Diocese, we have the task of laying a firm foundation for our Diocese — one that other bishops who shall come after us shall build on.

In order to achieve this, we shall strive to nurture our Christians into Christian maturity for the positive continuity of our diocese even when we are no more. I therefore call upon all people of God and well-wishers to join us as we lay the foundation of this new diocese for the glory of God.

"Come now, and let us reason together, says the Lord." — Isaiah 1:18

Bishop Tibaijuka has called upon Christians and clergy to embrace patriotism and hard work, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective effort in achieving the diocese's goals.

— Rt. Rev. Barnabas Tibaijuka
Pioneer Bishop, West Ruwenzori Diocese

Leadership Vision

The Bishop's Priorities

I

Unity

Fostering unity among Christians, clergy, and community leaders for the collective advancement of Bundibugyo District.

II

Hard Work & Patriotism

Encouraging Christians to dedicate themselves to hard work and national development as an expression of their faith.

III

Spiritual Maturity

Nurturing a spiritually mature flock that can sustain the diocese and pass on the faith to future generations.

IV

Community Development

Working with political leaders and the community to drive social and economic development across the region.

V

Pastoral Care

Making spiritual support accessible to every believer — especially those in remote and underserved areas of Bundibugyo.

VI

Laying the Foundation

Building strong institutional and spiritual foundations that future generations and future bishops can continue to grow.

Diocese Leadership

Our Clergy

West Ruwenzori Diocese is served by a dedicated team of priests and clergy committed to the mission of the Church across Bundibugyo District.

Under the leadership of Bishop Tibaijuka, the clergy work tirelessly in parishes, schools, and communities to spread the Gospel and serve God's people.
