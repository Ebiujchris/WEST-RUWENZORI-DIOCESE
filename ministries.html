Our Ministries

Serving God and the community across Bundibugyo District through faith, love, and action.

What We Do

Serving God & Community

The Diocese of West Ruwenzori ministers to the whole person — spiritually, physically, and socially.

I

Worship & Prayer

At the heart of our diocese is a vibrant culture of worship. Regular Sunday services, midweek prayer meetings, and Bible study groups meet across all parishes to grow the faith of every believer.

St. Barnabas Cathedral in Bumadu serves as the mother church, holding services that gather thousands of faithful from near and far.

II

Children & Youth Ministry

The next generation is our priority. Through Sunday school, confirmation classes, youth fellowships, and camps, we raise young believers grounded in the Word and ready to lead.

Bishop Tibaijuka has confirmed dozens of new members into the faith during pastoral visits, reflecting the diocese's commitment to growth from within.

III

Education

We believe education is a cornerstone of community transformation. The diocese actively supports schools and educational programs, equipping children and young people in Bundibugyo for a brighter, purpose-driven future.

IV

Community Development

Faith without action is incomplete. Our diocese partners with local government, NGOs, and community organizations to drive development — from water projects and agriculture to peace-building and family counseling.

The involvement of political leaders and MPs reflects the wide trust the diocese has earned in Bundibugyo.

Whether you want to volunteer, partner with us, or support our work financially, we'd love to hear from you.
