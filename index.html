Message from the Bishop

A Firm Foundation
for God's Glory

Receive Christian and lovely greetings from the Diocese of West Ruwenzori — from my wife Alice Tibaijuka, myself, our family, and the entire Christian community at large.

As pioneer bishop, we have the task of laying a firm foundation upon which future bishops shall build. We shall strive to nurture our Christians into spiritual maturity for the positive continuity of this diocese. I call upon all people of God and well-wishers to join us as we lay this foundation for the glory of God.

Our Work

Serving God & Community

The Diocese of West Ruwenzori is committed to spiritual growth, education, healthcare, and community development across Bundibugyo District.

I

Worship & Prayer

Regular services, prayer meetings, and Bible study across all parishes in the diocese.

II

Children & Youth

Nurturing the next generation through Sunday school, confirmation classes, and youth fellowships.

III

Education

Supporting schools and educational programs to equip the people of Bundibugyo for a brighter future.

IV

Healthcare

Partnering with health workers to bring medical care and wellness to remote communities.

V

Community Development

Empowering families and communities through outreach, counseling, and social support programs.

VI

Missions & Evangelism

Spreading the Gospel to every corner of Bundibugyo and beyond with passion and purpose.
